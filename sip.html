Plan Your Investments With a SIP Calculator

A SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) lets you invest a fixed amount every month into mutual funds. This SIP calculator for India helps you estimate the maturity value of your SIP, the total amount you invest over the years and the profit generated due to compounding.

Inputs Required

You only need three inputs ‚Äì monthly SIP amount, expected annual return (% per year) and investment period in years. The tool converts the annual return into a monthly rate and applies monthly compounding to estimate growth.

Understanding the Graphs

The first graph shows year-wise SIP growth, comparing total invested amount with the estimated value. The second graph shows how much of the final corpus is your own contribution and how much is profit. This makes it easier to visualise the power of long-term investing.
