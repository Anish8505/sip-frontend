About This Tax Calculator

This tool uses simple slabs for Old and New tax regimes plus 4% health & education cess based on your input income. It is for quick illustration and planning only.

Actual tax liability may change based on deductions, exemptions, surcharges, rebates and latest rules. Always cross-check with the official Income Tax portal or a Chartered Accountant before filing.

Income Tax Calculator for Salaried Indians

With this income tax calculator you can quickly estimate how much tax you may pay in a year under the Old or New tax regime. It also shows your net take-home income after tax and the effective tax rate in percentage.

This is particularly useful for comparing salary offers, planning investments and understanding the impact of switching between regimes.
