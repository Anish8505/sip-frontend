Yearly Expense vs Required Corpus

Assumptions

This calculator uses simple inflation and a 4% safe withdrawal rule (corpus ≈ 25 × yearly expenses) to estimate how much money you may need at retirement.

Steps used:
1. Inflate your current monthly expense till retirement using the inflation rate.
2. Convert that to yearly expense at retirement.
3. Multiply yearly expense by 25 to estimate corpus under a 4% withdrawal rule.

This is a rough rule-of-thumb. Actual needs depend on lifestyle, returns, other income (like pension) and how long you expect to be retired.
