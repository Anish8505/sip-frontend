Enter your one-time investment amount, expected annual return and investment period in years. Click “Calculate Lumpsum” to see invested amount, maturity value and profit. The charts show how your money grows year by year and how much of the final amount is principal vs profit.

How This Lumpsum Calculator Works

This calculator assumes a one-time investment that grows at a fixed annual return rate, compounded yearly. It’s designed for Indian investors to quickly estimate how their money could grow over time.

Formula used (lumpsum growth):
Future Value = Principal × (1 + R)n
where R is the annual return rate (in decimal) and n is the number of years.

The line chart compares the constant invested amount with the estimated value year by year. The doughnut chart splits the final corpus into principal and profit so you can clearly see how much compounding has worked for you.

This is an approximate model for educational use only. Actual returns depend on the mutual fund or asset you choose and market conditions. Please verify with official documents or a financial advisor before investing.

Lumpsum Investment Calculator for Indian Investors

A lumpsum investment means you invest a large amount in one shot, instead of making monthly SIP contributions. This calculator helps you understand how that amount can grow with compounding, based on simple assumptions.

Inputs

  • Investment amount (₹)
  • Expected annual return (%)
  • Investment duration in years

Outputs

  • Invested amount
  • Estimated maturity amount
  • Total profit
  • Interactive year-by-year growth chart
  • Invested vs profit composition chart
