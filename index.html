What is a SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)?

SIP allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly (monthly) in mutual funds. It helps average out your cost and builds wealth through compounding.

How our SIP calculator helps you

  • Enter monthly investment
  • Enter expected return rate
  • Enter number of years

The calculator instantly shows your invested amount, maturity value and total profit.

What is Lumpsum Investment?

In a lumpsum investment, you invest a one-time amount and let it grow over time based on market returns.

When should you choose SIP vs Lumpsum?

  • Choose SIP if you want disciplined monthly investing.
  • Choose Lumpsum if you have surplus money to invest at once.
  • Best approach: combine both for long-term wealth creation.
