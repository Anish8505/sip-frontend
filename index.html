All Indian Investment & Tax Calculators in One Place

This website provides separate, detailed pages for SIP, Lumpsum, FD, RD, PPF, EMI, Income Tax, CAGR, Retirement, NPS and SWP calculators. Each calculator has its own explanation, formula and graph so you can clearly understand how your money grows, how loans work and how much tax you may pay.

How to Use This Site

Choose a calculator from the cards above or the navigation bar. On each calculator page you can enter your numbers, see the results and view an interactive chart of growth or breakdown. These tools are built for Indian investors and salaried professionals.

Disclaimer

All calculators give approximate values only. Real returns, EMIs and tax liabilities depend on bank or fund rules, tax law changes and your personal situation. Please verify with official sources or a financial advisor before taking final decisions.
