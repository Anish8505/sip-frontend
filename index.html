Investment & Tax Calculators in One Place

This page brings together SIP, Lumpsum, FD, RD, EMI, Income Tax and CAGR calculators so you can quickly check how your money grows, what EMI you will pay and how much tax you may owe (approximate).

SIP (Systematic Investment Plan)

SIP allows you to invest a fixed amount regularly (usually monthly) in mutual funds. It helps average out your cost and builds wealth over time using the power of compounding.

Lumpsum Investment

In a lumpsum investment, you invest a one-time amount and let it grow for a number of years at a certain expected return. This is common when you receive a bonus, inheritance or sell an asset.

FD & RD

Fixed Deposit (FD) is a safe investment where you deposit a lump sum at a fixed interest rate. Recurring Deposit (RD) is similar to SIP at a bank where you deposit a fixed amount every month.

EMI (Equated Monthly Instalment)

EMI is the fixed amount you pay every month towards a loan such as a home loan, car loan or personal loan. Each EMI has both interest and principal. Our calculator shows EMI, total amount paid and total interest.

Income Tax Calculator (India)

The income tax calculator uses simple Indian slabs for Old and New regimes and adds 4% health & education cess. It shows approximate tax payable, net income after tax and effective tax rate.

CAGR (Compounded Annual Growth Rate)

CAGR tells you your average annual growth rate between a starting value and an ending value. It smooths out ups and downs and gives a single percentage that answers: “If my investment moved from A to B in N years, what was the yearly growth rate?”

Important Disclaimer

All calculators on this page provide approximate values based on the inputs you provide and simplified formulas. Actual returns, loan EMIs and tax liabilities may differ based on market conditions, bank or fund policies, tax rules, deductions and surcharges. Always verify with official sources or a qualified financial advisor before making final financial decisions.
